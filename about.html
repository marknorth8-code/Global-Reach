Our Story

Our core team originates from development management and consultancy backgrounds. After building and delivering our own development projects, we bring this experience back into our consultancy work, continuing to do what we do best.

Our core team has worked together in the same locations before expanding into the international markets where we now maintain offices. This shared foundation ensures strong alignment and a consistent, well-coordinated approach across our services.

Ashan Soysa

Ashan is an experienced Development Manager, based in Auckland. An NZRAB Registered Architect and Senior Project Manager, he has some 20 years of experience delivering high-quality fitout and new-build projects across commercial, government, retail, education, hospitality, and cultural sectors. Ashan combines design expertise with strong skills in project management, procurement, and contract administration.

Mark North

Mark has extensive property experience in the UK and overseas, including five years with Fletcher Building in New Zealand. He began his career at Colliers International in London, specialising in development appraisals, land valuations, and development strategy. He subsequently focused on development management, including new build projects, refurbishments, and large-scale portfolio integrations. Key assignments include steel and construction facilities for Fletcher, major church and community projects, and residential and commercial developments in both the UK and NZ. Mark holds an MA in Property Investment and Management and is a RICS member.

Oliver Aley

Oliver combines a strong IT background with practical property experience. He keeps the team up to date with the latest software and search tools, while providing tailored investment solutions for clients. Oliver began his career in customer service before progressing to Senior IT Technician, developing leadership, problem-solving, and operational management skills. Now a property consultant, he specialises in site searches, development appraisals, project management, and maximising returns for investors. His technical expertise and local market knowledge allow him to deliver data-driven, practical property advice.

Our Work

Beyond our consultancy services, Global Reach actively develops and delivers its own property projects across each market in which we operate. This direct involvement spans the full property cycle, from initial market analysis and acquisition through development, management, and exit.

By investing our own capital and working through the same challenges faced by our clients, we gain first-hand insight into local regulations, market dynamics, delivery risks, and long-term value creation. These hands-on experiences directly inform our advisory approach.
