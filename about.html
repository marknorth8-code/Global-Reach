Our Story

Our core team originates from development management and consultancy backgrounds. After building and delivering our own development projects, we bring this experience back into our consultancy work, continuing to do what we do best.

Our team has worked together in the same locations before expanding into the international markets where we now maintain offices. This shared foundation ensures strong alignment and a consistent, well-coordinated approach across our services.

Ashan Soysa

Ashan is an experienced Development Manager, based in Auckland. An NZRAB Registered Architect and Senior Project Manager, he has some 20 years of experience delivering high-quality fitout and new-build projects across commercial, government, retail, education, hospitality, and cultural sectors.

Mark North

Mark has extensive property experience in the UK and overseas, including five years with Fletcher Building in New Zealand. He began his career at Colliers International in London, specialising in development appraisals, land valuations, and development strategy. Mark holds an MA in Property Investment and Management and is a RICS member.
