About Peggy Hsu

Peggy Hsu, AIA

Principal

Peggy received her Bachelor of Architecture degree from California State Polytechnic University, Pomona where she spent an year studying abroad in Copenhagen, Denmark. Prior to establishing Hsu McCullough, Peggy worked at several architectural and real estate development companies where she fully guided design and construction projects ranging from single family residences, executive suites in office buildings, mixed-use condominium developments and boutiques restaurants located throughout Southern California. Peggy is a licensed architect in California and Hawaii.
