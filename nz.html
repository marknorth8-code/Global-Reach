Auckland, New Zealand

Why we like NZ for investment

Property in Auckland is expensive and it consistently ranks as one of the least affordable global cities relative to income. Population growth and shortage of supply are both factors. The Unitary Plan and zoning guides are designed to reduce much of the risk of new development projects and timing now might be right, the NZ dollar is low, broad economic signs are improving and major infrastructure like the City Rail Link are nearing completion.
