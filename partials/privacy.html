Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

At Katakano, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data across our global operations in the UK, Brazil, Japan, and New Zealand.

1. Data We Collect

  • Contact Information: When you use our contact form, we collect your name and email address to respond to your inquiry.
  • Technical Data: We automatically collect your IP address, browser type, and usage patterns via cookies to improve site performance and security.

2. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following legal frameworks:

  • UK & Brazil: Based on Legitimate Interest (for site security) and Consent (for contact forms and analytics).
  • New Zealand & Japan: In compliance with the Privacy Act 2020 and APPI regarding transparent data collection.
International Data Transfers: Your data may be stored or processed in servers located outside your home country. We ensure all transfers comply with standard contractual clauses (SCCs) to maintain protection levels equivalent to the UK GDPR and LGPD.

3. Your Regional Rights

Depending on your location, you have specific rights regarding your personal information:

  • UK (GDPR): Right to access, correct, or erase your data. Complaints can be made to the ICO.
  • Brazil (LGPD): Right to confirm processing and request anonymization. Contact our DPO at [Your Email].
  • Japan (APPI): Right to request disclosure of the purpose of use and cessation of use.
  • New Zealand: Right to access and request correction under the Privacy Commissioner guidelines.

4. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions for a period of 6 months unless a longer period is required for legal or business purposes.
