Exercise Your Data Rights

Under global privacy laws including the UK GDPR, Brazil's LGPD, Japan's APPI, and New Zealand's Privacy Act, you have specific rights regarding your personal information.

Right of Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you (often called a Subject Access Request or SAR).

Right to Erasure

Request that we delete your personal information from our systems, subject to legal retention requirements.

Right to Rectification

Ask us to update or correct any inaccurate or incomplete information we have about you.

Right to Object/Withdraw

Object to our processing of your data or withdraw consent you previously provided (e.g., for cookies or marketing).

How to Make a Request

To exercise any of the rights listed above, please send an email to our Data Protection team. To help us process your request quickly, please include:

  • Your full name.
  • The specific right you wish to exercise (e.g., "Request for Access").
  • The email address used to contact us previously (if applicable).
